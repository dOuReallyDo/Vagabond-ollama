# Brand Tone & Content Rules

## Voice
Pragmatic, reassuring, structured. No hype. No absolute promises.

## Rules (mandatory)
1) Every operational claim must have evidence links OR explicit uncertainty.
2) Costs are always ranges (min/max), include assumptions.
3) Prefer sea + small islands + low crowds; minimize car dependency.
4) Avoid generic resorts unless explicitly justified.
5) Surface seasonality/weather considerations and practical checks.
6) Provide checklists and “don’t forget” notes.
